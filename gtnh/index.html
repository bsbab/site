FAQ

Why daytime only/peaceful?

Mobs serve as more of an annoyance in GTNH. There are a large number of mob "difficulty" mods and configurations, so finding them all and disabling them is a challenge in and of itself. Even disabling nighttime and only having them spawn in caves/darkness is more bothersome than enjoyable.
Other than that, the darkness of night is obnoxious, and forcing players to jump into a bed every 12 minutes to be able to see is grating.


Why no weather?

Do you actually like the rain in Minecraft? The absurdly loud rain that makes it more difficult to see anything?
Rain also causes GregTech machines to explode, so even with rain disabled it may rain long enough to cause damage. Be wary.


How are quest rewards increased? Why?

All quest rewards that are a choice option have been changed to give all choices. There is also an exchange to turn basic lootbags into enchanted one versions that do not give junk items for free.
Getting rewarded for doing quests always feels good. Most quests feel like there is a lot of work to put into them, so I believe that the rewards should be high as well. Also, getting junk from a lootbag makes you feel like you wasted your time, and doing the enchantment for it with the normal method is a pain.


What new quests? What for?

New quests have been added to the coins quest tab that give different items and devices to make the game a little less painful and give more use for coins. A new quest tab for mob drops has also been added as a result of the change to peaceful difficulty.
